# E-Commerce Analytics with Olist Retail Data

This project analyzes real Brazilian e-commerce data from [Olist](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce), providing insights into customer behavior, seller performance, product sales, delivery times, and payment patterns.

The goal is to simulate the role of a data analyst in a retail e-commerce department by cleaning, merging, analyzing, and visualizing large volumes of transactional data using Python.
